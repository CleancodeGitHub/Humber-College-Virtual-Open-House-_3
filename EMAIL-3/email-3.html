A BIT ABOUT US

At the FMCA, we specialize in storytelling of all forms, no matter the medium. While we may not be able to welcome you in person, we have created a number of interactive and informative opportunities and experiences to help share with you all that our program has to offer: from hands-on experience in Live Labs to work-integrated learning all while learning industry-specific business skills needed wherever your career and your story take you.
